About

Born sometime between the late 1970s and mid-1980s, Maria remains a mysterious figure despite the numerous studies and biographies published to date, including the award-winning Netflix documentary "Woman with Scissors" about her life.

His best-known works include a vast work of collage on paper that recreates a personal universe in which natural history coexists with Victorian aesthetics and surrealism.

Among her works less known by the general public are drawings, tarot decks and a curious video game called "Bunnies Story", which is believed to have been the trigger for the mental problems that María began to manifest towards the middle of her life.

During her time as a web developer, which lasted about ten years, Maria contributed to the creation of successful applications used by companies and individuals around the world. The famous disagreements and rudeness between María and Jeff Bezos about the rights to use the "Buyer's Delights" tool by the Amazon company led her to follow in the footsteps of her admired Marak Squires and completely destroy the code that had been reported to her World Fame.

Currently María, away from technology and the public, lives with her family in the desert of Almería, completely dedicated to her tarot readings and the study of Kabbalah.
